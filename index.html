Choosing the Right Size

A well-sized rug has the power to anchor a room. It brings balance, defines zones, and allows your furniture to feel intentionally placed rather than floating.

Below, we've mapped out our core sizes to help you visualise how each one sits within your home.

Entry, Kitchen & Hallway Sizes
60 × 90 cm — Doormat

A welcoming layer at your front or back door. Perfect for adding warmth and texture to entry points.

80 × 200 cm — Short Runner

Ideal for kitchens or bathrooms. Place in front of a sink or vanity to soften hard surfaces and add comfort underfoot.

80 × 300 cm — Hallway Runner

Designed for longer hallways or galley kitchens. Helps visually elongate the space while adding a layered, collected feel.

Living, Bedroom & Outdoor Spaces
170 × 240 cm — Small Living Spaces

Best suited to smaller living areas or under a coffee table. We recommend placing the front legs of your furniture on the rug to create a cohesive zone.

240 × 310 cm — Standard Living Spaces

A versatile size for most living rooms. Large enough to anchor your seating arrangement, allowing all key furniture pieces to sit comfortably on the rug.

275 × 365 cm — Large Spaces

For expansive living areas or open-plan spaces. This size creates a beautifully grounded foundation, giving the room a more elevated, finished feel.

Dining Room Sizing
170 × 240 cm

Suitable for smaller dining tables (4–6 seats). Chairs may sit partially off when pulled out.

240 × 310 cm

Ideal for 6–8 seat tables. Allows chairs to remain on the rug when in use.

275 × 365 cm

Perfect for larger tables (8+ seats). Ensures ample space for movement while maintaining proportion.

What is PET?

Our rugs are crafted from PET yarn, a fibre made from recycled plastic bottles. Designed to feel soft underfoot while offering exceptional durability, PET is what allows these rugs to transition seamlessly between indoor and outdoor spaces.

Unlike traditional outdoor materials, PET rugs have a more natural, textural finish — making them just as suited to living rooms and bedrooms as they are to patios and verandas.

Where can it be used?

Indoors, anywhere you would typically place a rug — living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and entryways.

Outdoors, our rugs perform beautifully in covered spaces such as verandas, and alfresco areas. Given the unpredictability of the Australian climate, we recommend placing your rug under adequate overhead cover to protect it from heavy or prolonged exposure to the elements.

Care & Maintenance
1. Regular Cleaning

Vacuum regularly to remove dirt and debris.

2. Spot Cleaning

Clean spills promptly using mild detergent and water.

3. Deep Cleaning

For a more thorough clean, hose down outdoors and allow to air dry completely.

4. Outdoor Care

Avoid prolonged exposure to heavy rain. Ensure rugs are placed in covered areas where possible.

5. Rotation

Rotate periodically to ensure even wear, particularly in high-traffic areas.

6. Storage

If storing, ensure the rug is completely dry before rolling and placing in a cool, dry space.
